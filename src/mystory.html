University of Sydney - Bachelor of Science (2016-2022)

I started my study in the summer of 2016, commencing a Bachelor of Medical Science degree with the hopes of pursuing a career in Medicine. However, after some consideration, I realised that that path wasn't meant for me. As such, I transferred to a Bachelor of Science, and began slowly working towards its completion. After a few slow years, I graduated December 2022.

White Cockatoo Hotel - Host (2019-Present)

Parallel to my studies, I worked in a local pub - The White Cockatoo Hotel. This helped to break up my time and provide much needed novelty and diversity. While my time at uni taught me diligence, discipline and wider skills through academia, The White Cockatoo instilled in me a keen ability to communicate, to cooperate and manage not only my time and work, but the expectations of my patrons as well.

Loan Base, Mortgage Broker’s Assistant (2020)

In 2020, I commenced an internship at Loan Base, an independent mortgage broking company, and steadily built up skills before advancing to permanent part-time employment. My role primarily revolved around administrative work related to loan processing. I assisted and helped execute different stages of loan processing, moving them from initial point of contact to full approval, liaising with lenders, clients and regulatory bodies.

Gorilla Jobs Recruitment - Recruitment Associate (2022-2023)

While my graduation took place in December 2022, I had actually finished my classes in June 2022, freeing up my time and providing an opportunity to diversify my skill set. In July 2022, I decided to try my hand at recruitment at Gorilla Jobs. There, I recruited Lawyers for firms in Victoria and Queensland, and gained an understanding of human resources: talent identification, acquisition and retention.

Current Path - Present

And now, here we are. I’ve since begun honing my skills in web development, and see myself continuing down this path. Feel free to peruse some of my projects on Github, and feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like to chat!
